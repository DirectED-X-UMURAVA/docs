## Vision
An Africa where digital talents are abundant, skilled, empowered, and fully integrated into the global job market and Digital Economy.

## Mission
To build Africa's largest workforce for the digital economy - boosting business productivity and growth while creating thousands of decent jobs for African Talents. We exist to bridge the global talent shortage and reduce unemployment in the most in-demand digital career fields.

## Purpose
Making Hiring and Outsourcing Faster and Easier

## Tagline
Find Africa's Vetted Talents, On-Demand.
