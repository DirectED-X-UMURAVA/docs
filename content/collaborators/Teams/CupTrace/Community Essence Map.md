**CupTrace (Cardano Coffee Traceability)**

_“We want to know where our coffee goes—and why our income never grows.”_

---

## COMMUNITY IDENTITY

CupTrace represents people across the coffee value chain who believe in honest work, shared value, and dignity. From smallholder farmers in Rwanda’s green hills to international roasters crafting the perfect cup, this community blends tradition with innovation. They are united by a shared commitment to quality, fairness, and long-term sustainability.

---

## CORE NEEDS

- End-to-end traceability from farm to final cup
    
- Verifiable fairness in pricing and sourcing
    
- Visibility into the journey, data, and story of every coffee batch
    
- Tools that empower farmers rather than burden them
    
- A trusted global connection between origin communities and consumers
    

---

## PAIN POINTS

- Hidden processes and inconsistent reporting across the supply chain
    
- Weak or unverifiable fair-trade and sustainability claims
    
- Farmers excluded from data access and decision-making
    
- International buyers lacking proof of origin and quality
    
- Consumers uncertain about who and what to trust
    

---

## SHARED VALUES

- **Transparency** — Everyone deserves access to the truth behind the bean
    
- **Fairness** — Value should follow effort; farmers should not operate in the dark
    
- **Quality** — Coffee is a craft, and every stage deserves recognition
    
- **Trust** — Strong relationships are built through accountability
    
- **Sustainability** — Ethical production ensures coffee can thrive for generations
    

---

## ASPIRATIONS

- A supply chain where transparency is the standard, not the exception
    
- A system where value flows fairly back to origin communities
    
- Empowered farmers who own their data, identity, and story
    
- Confident buyers and consumers supported by verified proof
    
- A future where technology restores trust, equity, and dignity in coffee
